Juneli Restaurant & Bar

We offer dining in the Juneli Restaurant, in The PALM TREE Garden, at our Over the Moon Rooftop Lounge, and outside on The Rooftop itself. Groups/seminars may choose to dine in the Atithi Conference Hall. The Juneli Bar is a popular ‘watering hole’, offering a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, with its own seating area.
